Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Evan Tidd ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with SMS text messaging services.

SMS Program Description

We operate a personal SMS-based assistant service. SMS messages are used exclusively for transactional and operational purposes including personal task management, reminders, and assistant-style notifications. We do not send marketing or promotional SMS messages.

How You Opt In

SMS messaging is available only to individuals who have registered a mobile telephone number through the Veda or Taskbook applications using an authenticated account, after checking the SMS consent checkbox in Taskbook Settings. You must then text START to the Veda SMS number from your registered number to complete enrollment and expressly consent to receive SMS messages as part of this assistant service. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

How to Opt Out

To stop receiving messages: Reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. To resume service, you must re-register through the Veda or Taskbook app.

You may also opt out by sending any of the following keywords: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message, or contact us directly at help@mrtiddles.com.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with the service. Messages are sent only in direct response to your own text messages or as part of explicitly requested reminders and notifications.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your plan.

Information We Collect

When you use our SMS service, we may collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • The content of messages you send to us
  • Timestamps and interaction metadata

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through SMS is used solely to:

  • Process and respond to your requests
  • Deliver the personal assistant functionality you have requested
  • Maintain a record of your preferences and active tasks

We do not use your information for marketing purposes or to send unsolicited messages.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your phone number or message content with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may use service providers (such as Twilio for message delivery) who are contractually bound to protect your information and prohibited from using it for any purpose other than providing the service.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared, sold, or transferred to third parties or lead generators for any purpose.

Data Retention

Message content and interaction data are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@mrtiddles.com.
