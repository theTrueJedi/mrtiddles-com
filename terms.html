Program Description

Program name: Veda SMS Messaging

Veda SMS Messaging is a personal assistant service delivered via SMS. Messages are used exclusively for transactional and operational purposes including task management, reminders, and assistant-style notifications. We do not send marketing or promotional messages.

Eligibility & Enrollment

This service is available only to individuals who have registered a mobile telephone number through the Veda or Taskbook applications using an authenticated account, after checking the SMS consent checkbox in Taskbook Settings. Enrollment is confirmed by texting START to the Veda SMS number from your registered number.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with the service. Messages are sent only in direct response to your own text messages or as part of explicitly requested reminders and notifications.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your plan.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for an automated response with support information, or contact us directly at help@mrtiddles.com.

How to Opt Out

To stop receiving messages: Reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent. To resume service, you must re-register through the Veda or Taskbook app.

You may also opt out by sending any of the following keywords: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT.
