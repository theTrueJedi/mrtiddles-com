Veda

AI-Powered Personal Messaging Assistant

Veda is a personal AI assistant that lives in your SMS inbox. Send a text message to manage your to-do list, set reminders, and interact with your apps — no app to open, no screen to unlock. Just your phone's native messaging thread.

What Veda Does

Veda acts as an intelligent interface between you and the tools you use every day. Instead of jumping between apps, you send natural-language messages to Veda and it handles the rest.

  • Personal assistance — create, organize, and complete tasks with a simple text message
  • AI-powered triage — Veda reads your intent and routes each message to the right action automatically
  • App orchestration — integrates with custom apps like Taskbook to manage data across your personal toolset
  • Smart reminders — schedule follow-ups and time-based nudges delivered directly to your messages

How It Works

Every message you send is processed by a large language model that understands your intent and executes the right action. Veda maintains context across your conversation so you can refer back to previous tasks, adjust priorities, and get status updates — all in natural language.

On the backend, Veda connects to your authenticated apps and performs real operations: creating tasks, marking items complete, fetching lists, or triggering custom workflows. Responses are concise and returned directly to your SMS thread, typically within seconds.

SMS Messaging Program

Veda SMS Messaging is a transactional service operated by Evan Tidd (Design & Technology Services). Messages are sent exclusively in direct response to your requests or as part of explicitly configured reminders and notifications. No marketing or promotional messages are ever sent.

How to opt in: Register your mobile number through the Taskbook application at taskbook.mrtiddles.com. A confirmation SMS is sent immediately — reply YES to activate the service and provide your express written consent. No messages are sent until YES is received.

To stop receiving messages: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent. To resume, re-register through the Taskbook app.

  • Help: Reply HELP to any message, or email help@mrtiddles.com
  • Message frequency varies based on your interactions — messages are sent only in response to your texts or configured reminders
  • Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details.
  • Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared, sold, or transferred to third parties or lead generators for any purpose.
